template<class T_BaseClass>
class pmacc::BorderMapping< T_BaseClass >

This maps onto the border to 1 exchange direction (e.g.

TOP, BOTTOM, TOP + LEFT, ...) Choosing multiple directions defines an intersection [1] in mathematical set theory. The area is basically the same as the surrounding guard region but on the border.

Examples: FRONT: Whole top area of the border (Size: ~x*y) FRONT + LEFT: Only the edge at the intersection of the front and left border (Size: ~y) FRONT + LEFT + TOP: Only the corner super cell(s) (Size: ~1)

[1] https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Intersection_%28set_theory%29
