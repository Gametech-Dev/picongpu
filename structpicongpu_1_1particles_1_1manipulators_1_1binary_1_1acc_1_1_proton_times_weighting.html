Increase weighting of particleDest by proton number of SrcParticle.

The frame's numberOfProtonsof T_SrcParticle is used to increase the weighting of particleDest. Useful to increase the weighting of macro electrons when cloned from an ion with Z>1. Otherwise one would need Z macro electrons (each with the same weighting as the initial ion) to keep the charge of a pre-ionized atom neutral.
