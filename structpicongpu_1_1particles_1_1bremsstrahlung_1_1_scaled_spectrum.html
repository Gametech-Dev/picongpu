Detailed Description

Generates and holds the lookup tables for the scaled differential cross section and the stopping power.

scaled differential cross section = electron energy loss times cross section per unit energy

stopping power = energy loss per unit length

The lookup tables are generated from the screened Bethe-Heitler cross section. See e.g.: Salvat, F., et al. "Monte Carlo simulation of bremsstrahlung emission by electrons." Radiation Physics and Chemistry 75.10 (2006): 1201-1219.
